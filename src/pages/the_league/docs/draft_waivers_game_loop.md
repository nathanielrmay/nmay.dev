## Initial Draft & League Year

The initial draft will be held on **June 27, 2026**. The first lineup lock occurs **Sunday, July 5, 2026**. The week between the draft and the first lock is a dead period — no stats are collected and no lineup changes are processed. This window is reserved for the commissioner to verify all systems are in order before the league goes live.

The stat collection period runs from **July 5, 2026 – July 4, 2027** for League Year 1.

### Split-Season Stat Collection

Leagues whose real-world seasons are split across two calendar years will pull stats from multiple league years. The League grades categories using each sport's stats from the corresponding real-world season:

- **MLB:** Stats from MLB season 2026 (Jul–Oct), then MLB season 2027 (Mar–Jun)
- **MLS:** Stats from MLS season 2026 (Jul–Oct), then MLS season 2027 (Mar–May)
- **NBA Playoffs:** Late-season NBA playoff games may span into a new NBA season year

**Example:** A player hits 45 total bases during the MLB 2026 season (Jul–Sep) and 60 total bases during the MLB 2027 season (Mar–Jun). His League Year 1 total bases would be **105**, combining both real-world MLB seasons within the League year window.

## Lineup Locks

Lineup changes are processed weekly on Sundays. During a lineup lock, adds/drops are not processed — you may only rearrange your lineup using currently rostered players.

## Monthly Re-Draft / Waivers

Re-drafts (waivers) occur on the first Saturday of each month. You are free to drop and add as many players as you like. For each player kept, you forfeit a draft pick starting from your last pick and working upward.

**Example — 40-round monthly re-draft:**

| Players Kept | Picks Forfeited | Picks Remaining | Picks Retained |
| ------------ | --------------- | --------------- | -------------- |
| 0            | None            | 40              | Rounds 1–40    |
| 1            | Round 40        | 39              | Rounds 1–39    |
| 2            | Rounds 40, 39   | 38              | Rounds 1–38    |
| 10           | Rounds 40–31    | 30              | Rounds 1–30    |
| 20           | Rounds 40–21    | 20              | Rounds 1–20    |
| 30           | Rounds 40–11    | 10              | Rounds 1–10    |

Keeping 30 players means you enter the re-draft with only picks 1–10.
